# Welcome

This post exists so the blog has safe placeholder content after the original personal posts are removed.

## What to replace first

1. Update `frosti.config.yaml`.
2. Rewrite the pages in `src/pages/`.
3. Replace these sample posts with your own writing.

## Why this matters

Templates should be easy to fork without leaking the previous owner's identity, resume, links, or unpublished context.
